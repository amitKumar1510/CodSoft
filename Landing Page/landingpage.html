Welcome to the world of Univote.

Univote is an eVoting Web Application. This eVoting site is a website or platform that enables eligible voters to cast their votes electronically in an election inside the campus. The purpose of an e-voting site is to provide a convenient, secure, and efficient way for voters to participate in the electoral process, without the need to physically visit a polling station.

High-quality Design

Our eVoting website is meticulously designed to redefine the electoral process, ensuring a seamless, secure, and accessible voting experience for users. The design embodies a harmonious blend of functionality, user-centricity, and aesthetic appeal.

Increasing voter turnout

By making it easier and more convenient to vote, an e-voting site can help increase voter turnout, particularly among groups that may have difficulty accessing polling stations, such as people with disabilities or those who live in remote areas.

Enhancing election security

An eVoting site can incorporate various security measures to protect against fraud, or other types of interference. For example, the site may use multi-factor authentication, and other safeguards to ensure that only authorized voters can cast their ballots.
